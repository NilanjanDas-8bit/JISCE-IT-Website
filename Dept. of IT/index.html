the Department of Information Technology

Why Choose Information Technology

Under the Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), West Bengal, JISCE is an NIRF-ranked and NAAC A-graded institution. NIPU, the World Bank Group, UNESCO, MHRD, AICTE, and India's First Flipped Institute Digital studio, well-equipped laboratories, and smart classrooms.

BENEFITS OF TAKING IT AS CAREER

IT careers offer innovation, remote work, creativity, and global opportunities but demand adaptability in a fast-paced field and lifelong learning. Ideal for tech and problem-solving enthusiasts, IT is highly rewarding.

NEW TECHNOLOGY

IT innovation drives tech evolution via software, hardware, AI, cloud, and cybersecurity, shaping our digital future with efficiency gains, improved user experiences, and new industries/jobs. Rapid pace keeps IT dynamic for pros and users.

SUPPORTIVE FACULTIES

Friendly IT college faculties create a positive learning environment, offering approachability, support, and effective communication. They boost the educational experience, motivating students to excel in IT with confidence.
